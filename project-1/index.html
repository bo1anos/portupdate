About Us

Our team is made up of professionals from different backgrounds and interests, with hopes to pivot into the field of web development. Currently, we are enrolled part-time in Georgia Tech’s Full Stack Coding Boot Camp. Collectively, we have a wide range of experiences from the food industry, international education, manual labor, and the tech field. Although we may have different life experiences, we came together with a common goal to create the BougieFoodie. We hope you enjoy our project!

Why BougieFoodie?

The concept came about after we discovered our team’s deep passion for food and social media. We wanted to design a space where the beginner and expert cooks could not only search for their favorite recipes but watch how the meal is prepared while learning the origin/history of the dish simultaneously.
